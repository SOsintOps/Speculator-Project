# Icon Set — Gemini Image Generation Prompts

Style: Elementary (TV series) — mysterious detective figures, OSINT aesthetic.
Never show a full face. Consistent dark palette across all icons.
Format: square 512x512 PNG.

---

## Base Prompt (prepend to every individual prompt)

> Style: dark, moody digital illustration. Film noir aesthetic inspired by CBS Elementary TV series. Muted color palette: deep navy, charcoal, amber accents. Character shown from behind, in silhouette, or with face obscured by shadow/hat/hood. Never show a full face. Minimalist background. Square format, 512x512px, transparent or dark background. Flat icon style suitable for a desktop launcher.

---

## 1. user.png — Hub principale (persona)

> [base prompt] A detective figure seen from behind, wearing a long dark coat, standing before a wall covered in pinned photos, red strings connecting them. Amber desk lamp glow. The figure's face is completely hidden.

## 2. domains.png — Domain/infrastruttura

> [base prompt] A gloved hand holding a magnifying glass over a dark world map with glowing network nodes and connection lines. No face visible. Cyan and amber highlights on the nodes.

## 3. instagram.png — Instagram OSINT

> [base prompt] A hooded figure in silhouette looking at a phone screen that casts a warm orange-pink glow onto their hidden face. The screen shows a grid of small photo thumbnails. Camera lens reflection visible.

## 4. reddit.png — Reddit OSINT

> [base prompt] A dark figure sitting at a desk, backlit by a monitor showing cascading text threads in orange. The figure is seen from behind, hunched over the keyboard. Orange ambient glow.

## 5. video.png — Video download

> [base prompt] A silhouetted hand pressing play on an old-style film projector. Film reels casting dramatic shadows on the wall. Warm amber light from the projector beam. No face visible.

## 6. archives.png — Web archives

> [base prompt] An open vintage filing cabinet in a dark room, with glowing digital files floating out of the drawers. A figure's gloved hand reaching for a file. Blue-white glow. No face.

## 7. image.png — Image/metadata

> [base prompt] A magnifying glass hovering over a photograph on a dark desk, revealing hidden EXIF data as glowing green text floating above the image. Gloved hand holding the magnifier. No face.

## 8. frameworks.png — OSINT frameworks

> [base prompt] A dark command center with multiple monitors showing different dashboards, maps, and data feeds. An empty chair in front. Seen from above/behind. Green and cyan screen glow. No person visible.

## 9. update.png — Update tools

> [base prompt] A dark workbench with mechanical gears and circuit boards being assembled by gloved hands. Small sparks flying. Amber light from a soldering iron. No face visible. Progress bar glowing on a small screen.

## 10. maigret.png — Maigret web UI

> [base prompt] A detective's desk with a vintage pipe, an open dossier, and a laptop showing a network graph with connected nodes. Warm amber desk lamp. The detective is just a shadow in the background. Pipe smoke curling upward.

## 11. api.png — API/recon tools
